THIS ISN'T A BRAND, IT'S A WAY OF MOVING

We’re a young crew with a short but steep trajectory. Grizzstorm was born from the need to document snowboarding without filters: raw, sharp, and intentional. We follow what drives the sport, support the riders shaping it, and capture the moments that speak louder than hype.

We also create gear that holds up where it matters: out there, in the cold, on the line. Built to hit. Designed for storms.

We ride with people who share that energy—on snow or off.

IF IT DROPS HARD, WE'LL BE THERE

At Grizzstorm, we don't just follow the big names—we follow the energy that's rising. We believe some of the most authentic forms of riding happen at independent events, built by people who ride first and organize later. That's where the future is moving.


We've backed underground comps, local throwdowns, and wild formats that don't care about points—only progression. If your event creates space for real riding, we want to see it.


If you think your comp carries that same Grizzstorm spirit—raw, focused, driven by intention—then reach out. Show us the mountain, the format, the vibe. If it speaks our language, we’ll be there.
